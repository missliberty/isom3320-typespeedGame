Game description

Are you ready to test your typing skills?

Meet the Typespeed challenge. When the game starts, you will see different words appear on screen and you need to use your keyboard to type them into the console. The game is case-senstive so make sure you don't have that CAPS LOCK ON! You will score points for each word typed in correctly - and lose them if you press ENTER with a typo.

Good luck!

Game Demo


Game Rules

Controls
  1. Press to submit your answer and score points.
  2. Press to fix typos before submission.
  3. When your word is matched with the word on the screen, the Donkey Kong will destroy your word and your score will go up!
  4. Once you correctly guess 5 words, the difficulty of the game increases!
Scoring Points

    The number of points you scored will be the same as the the length of the word you correctly typed, e.g. cone: 4 points, banana: 6 points

    Note: All words are pre-loaded from a static library located in the game folder, called words.txt.

The Leaderboard

All the scores are saved when the game finishes and you can see which player is in the lead so far!
